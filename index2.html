About & Contact

I’m Nicholas, an indie developer who loves mixing 80s arcade energy with modern game design. This page is a small hub for my experiments—prototypes, polished releases, and everything in between.


Every game here lives on itch.io, so you can follow, rate, and leave feedback there. I’m always iterating, so builds may change, break, and (hopefully) get better over time.


If you enjoy something, tell me. If something feels off, definitely tell me. That’s how the next game gets better.
